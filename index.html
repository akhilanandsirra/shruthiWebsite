Shruthi-2020

“The only way to discover the limits of the possible is to go beyond the impossible” From Transformation to Integration and now Innovation, the most awaited occasion of the year CBIT looks up to, “Shruthi 2020” has been evolving into a bigger and better celebration. With the cultural, literary & sports events on board, Shruthi-2020 is kicking off this 27th February 2020 to 29th February 2020. The most awaited cultural fest themed “Innovation” is coming up with an initiative “Promote Handloom-Reduce Plastic” and much more. Brace yourselves for this year’s avant-garde, “Shruthi-2020”
